FAQs

Do you replace our processor?

No. As an ISO we connect you to processors and let you route traffic. Keep your existing relationship or add new ones.

Which payment methods are supported?

Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover, debit (regulated/unregulated), ACH/eCheck, and pay‑by‑link.

Can you help reduce effective rates?

Often, via routing strategy, card‑present optimizations, and fraud controls. Actual savings depend on mix and agreements.

How quickly can we go live?

Onboarding is typically days, subject to underwriting and integration scope.
